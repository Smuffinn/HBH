About HarborHop

Your Gateway to Extraordinary Maritime Adventures

Our Journey Through Time

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate maritime enthusiasts, HarborHop began as a small startup in a coastal town of Cebu. What started as a simple dream to connect island communities has evolved into the Philippines' leading maritime booking platform.

Our founders, inspired by the traditional Filipino concept of 'bayanihan,' noticed the challenges faced by travelers and locals alike in securing boat tickets. Many would spend hours in long queues or struggle with unreliable booking systems. This sparked the idea of creating a modern, efficient solution that would revolutionize maritime travel.

Through years of dedication and innovation, we've grown from serving just three routes to connecting over 50 destinations across the Philippine archipelago. Our commitment to excellence has earned us multiple awards, including the 'Digital Innovation of the Year' in 2019 and 'Best Travel Tech Platform' in 2021.
